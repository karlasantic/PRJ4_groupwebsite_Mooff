The explanation fo the Mooff map

Experience a safe feeling during your (nightly) walks with Mooff Maps. Our innovative map shows you busy areas, guides you through well-lit paths and alerts you ahead of time of situations close by, when needed. Mooff into your walks with confidence, knowing we will Mooff with you.

How it works? That is simple. Based on your location and preferred walking distance, the Mooff Map will create a personalised route. Whether you are looking for a luminous path to walk on or an area where more dog owners are present, the Mooff Map will point it all out to you.
